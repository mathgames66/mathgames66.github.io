iScribble

iScribble.IO is an entertaining io game. In this game, you can release stress easily by drawing lines freely while competing with other players to survive.

Are you tired of the busy life in which you go to study or work in the morning and you come back home in the evening? You are looking for an entertaining game to help you relax and release stress after a long workday, right? Come to this game, iScribble.IO, to beat stress and make your desire come true!

Unlike other games with adventurous gameplay, iScribble.IO brings you entertainment thanks to simple mechanics, easy control, the ability when drawing according to your interest, and excitement when competing with other players. In this game, you can participate in a multiplayer competition to choose a person having the largest land and being able to survive as long as possible. Let's join and enjoy iScribble.IO!

Your mission is to control your square to draw a line-shaped tail and mark your area. You need to create closed circles to make these playing fields belong to your own. To attack other players, you can crash into their tails when they have not finished drawing a closed circle. In contrast, be careful when other players approach you. They can trap you to crash into your tail and eliminate you.

Use the minimap to follow other players on the playing field. Besides that, there is a game leaderboard on the top-right screen for you to check your rank and the people in the top rank. Try to expand your land as big as possible to create an admirable record! Moreover, after each round, you can go after your achievements and playing time to see your controlling ability and competing bravery.

iScribble.IO has eye-catching graphics, superb effects, and highly competitive gameplay. Besides that, you can type your name and select your favorite color for your character. All these features will bring you wonderful moments to get rid of boredom and stress. Have a great time!

Features of iScribble.IO

  • Colorful, vivid graphics and cool art animations
  • Use the minimap and leaderboard to increase your rank easily
  • Competitive drawing game with multiplayer
  • Simple mechanics and easy controls
  • Select the favorite color and name for your character
  • Freedom to draw closed circles before being attacked

Platform

  • iScribble.IO is available on both PC and mobile on our website browser.

How to control

  • Slide your mouse to move your character.
